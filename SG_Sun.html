Sun Sign

Just as the sun is the center of our solar system, so is your sun sign the center of your astrological chart around which all other placements revolve. Your sun sign is likely the part of your chart you know the best and is the common answer to, "What's your sign?" and is determined by your birthdate. This means that your sun sign is dependent on what astrological constellation was prominent in the sky on your date of birth. You may or may not strongly identify with the characteristics of this sign, but remember, sun sign is still just one piece of the star chart. This is, however, the first piece of your Big Three, which is comprised of sun, moon, and rising signs. Learn more about moon and rising signs at the links in the navigation bar above. My sun sign is in Saggittarius, the 9th sign in the astrological order. Saggittarius dates range from November 22nd - December 21st. My birthday is December 8th, 1991 placing me solidly in the center of the sign's season.

Saggittarius Basics:

  • Sag is ruled by the planet Jupiter
  • Jupiter also rules the sign of Picses
  • The symbol for Saggittarius is the archer or the centaur, or a centaur who is also an archer
  • Sag is the final fire sign in the astrological chart, preceeded by Aries and Leo

Saggittarius Traits:

  • Sag is categorized as the wanderer and/or the philosopher
  • The centaur that represents Saggittarius is named Chiron
  • Like the centaur we are represented by, Sag's are known to be loyal, optimistic, and very independent
  • We are also known as the cheerleader sign, as well as very optimistic
  • Eternal optimism can also lead to always searching for the next adventure, hobby, or connection, which can lead to a reputation for flakiness by those who do not understand a Sag's need to roam
  • Saggittarius is also known to be a class clown or a playful rebel of sorts, as well as blunt, sometimes speaking before a thought is fully formed or rambling on during storytelling
