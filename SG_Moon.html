Moon Sign

Just as the moon is the force behind the ebbs and flows of our oceans, so does your moon sign push and pull at our internal, emotional seas. Your sun sign is likely how you experience yourself; your thoughts and feelings that others may or may not be externally shared. Your moon sign is determined by your where and when you were born and correlates to the astrological sign that the moon was passing through at the time and place you were born. This means that your moon sign is dependent on the lunar cycle. Your moon sign is the second piece of your Big Three, which is comprised of sun, moon, and rising signs. My moon sign is in Capricorn, the 10th sign in the astrological order. Capricorn seasos is from December 22nd - January 20th.

Capricorn Basics:

  • Capricorn is ruled by the planet Saturn
  • The symbol for Capricorn is the sea goat
  • Cap is the last of the earth signs, preceeded by Taurus and Virgo

Capricorn Traits:

  • Capricorns value tradition and are inclined towards high standards and are grounded in ambition
  • Caps are also more inclined towards analysis than emotion
  • Like all earth signs, Caps have a reputation for stubbornness, but are also known for being responsible and hard working
  • Because they are inclined towards analyisis, this generally means processing their own emotions can be a challenge
